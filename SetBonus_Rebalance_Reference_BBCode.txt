[size=5][b]✦ Conditional Rebalance — Full Spell Reference[/b][/size][/center]
The optional [b]Conditional Rebalance[/b] submodule replaces every set's effects with the balance below. Each set keeps a lean always-on identity and gains [b]situational spikes[/b] that apply only while a state holds (shown in [i]italics[/i]); a few weakness drawbacks are conditional too. Default tiers at [b]2 / 4 / 6+[/b] pieces. "/s" = per-second Restore. Both magnitude sliders still apply. Click to expand.

[b]Conditions at a glance:[/b] [i]bloodied[/i] HP < 50% · [i]last stand[/i] HP < 25% · [i]unscathed[/i] HP > 75% · [i]winded[/i] FP < 50% · [i]fresh[/i] FP > 75% · [i]charged[/i] MP > 50% · [i]drained[/i] MP < 25% · night / day · sun up/down (up = daytime and outdoors) · indoors / outdoors · region · race · faction rank · bounty · birthsign · werewolf form · weapon stance · level & skill/attribute thresholds, singly or combined.
